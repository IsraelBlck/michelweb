Michel Edwar Mickael

Michel Edwar Mickael holds an MSc from Queen Mary University in 2006 and a PhD from Durham University in 2013. Currently serving as an Assistant Professor at IGHZ in Poland, he specializes in the intersection of immunology, molecular psychology, evolutionary biology, and AI. With over 40 publications to his name, Michel is a prolific researcher and a recognized expert in his field. Notably, he possesses exceptional language skills, communicating fluently in more than five languages.
