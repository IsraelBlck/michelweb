About Us

Our Mission

Neurological disorders encompass a wide range of diseases that affect the central nervous system, including the brain and spinal cord. A common symptom among neurological disorders is the impaired ability of patients to communicate with their environment, which can vary in severity. As a company grounded in academic research, we develop AI solutions focused on regenerating communication abilities, helping to re-establish lost connections with the environment. To achieve this, we focus on various aspects of neurological diseases, particularly the linguistic capabilities of the brain and how they are affected differently across various types of neurological conditions. If you are a patient suffering from these diseases or caring for someone who is, we would love to hear from you.
